About Us

Nestled in the heart of Hyde Park Village, Furry Baby was born from a simple belief: our pets are family—and they deserve to be celebrated in style. What began as a passion project by a devoted pet lover with an eye for design has grown into Tampa’s premier destination for upscale pet essentials.


At Furry Baby, we curate with care. From luxe collars, plush loungers, and playful toys every item in our boutique is chosen for its quality, charm, and soul. We partner with ethical brands and artisans who share our love for animals and our commitment to conscious luxury.


But we’re more than a boutique—we’re a community. A place where wagging tails meet warm smiles, and where every visit feels like a treat. Whether you’re shopping for your pup’s next statement piece or just stopping by for a belly rub, we’re here to make you feel at home.
